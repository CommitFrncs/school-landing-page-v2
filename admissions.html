Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for admission?

+

For Nursery 1, children must be at least 3 years old by September 1st. For Primary 1, children must be at least 6 years old by September 1st.

Is there an entrance examination?

+

Yes, we conduct age-appropriate assessments for students applying to Primary 2 and above. The assessment evaluates basic literacy, numeracy, and social readiness.

Do you offer scholarships?

+

We offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students and need-based financial assistance. Please contact our admissions office for more information.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

+

We maintain a low teacher-to-student ratio of 1:15 in primary classes and 1:20 in secondary classes to ensure personalized attention for each student.

Do you provide transportation services?

+

Yes, we offer safe and reliable school bus services covering major areas in Lagos. Transportation fees are separate from tuition fees.
